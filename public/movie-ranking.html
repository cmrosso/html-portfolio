The Best Funny Movies According to my son

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bridesmaids (2011)

A female-led comedy that broke stereotypes, focusing on friendship and the chaos leading up to a wedding, with memorable performances by Kristen Wiig.

The Big Lebowski (1998)

A cult classic that blends crime, bowling, and a case of mistaken identity. The Dude, played by Jeff Bridges, has become an iconic character.

Ghostbusters (1984)

A supernatural comedy that's both funny and exciting, following a team of scientists turned ghost catchers in New York City.
